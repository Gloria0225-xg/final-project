About this graph

The column plot above presented showcases the relationship between the average price and the color of products. The analysis focuses on three major categories: Clothings, Shoes and Accessories.To control the visualization, a convenient slider is provided, allowing users to adjust the price range displayed on the graph. By examining the plot, we can gain insights into how the average price varies across different colors within each category. This information enables us to identify any potential pricing patterns or trends associated with specific colors and categories. Such observations can assist in making informed decisions related to product pricing, inventory management, and marketing strategies.
